OUR SERVICES

This is What we do;

Carpet Cleaning

Carpet cleaning services are essential for maintaining the cleanliness and hygiene of indoor spaces, and they can encompass a variety of cleaning methods and techniques to meet the needs of your customers.

Engine Wash

This service is primarily cosmetic and helps improve the appearance of the engine, but it can also have some practical benefits, such as identifying and addressing potential issues and making routine maintenance tasks more accessible.

Body Wash

Full-service carwashes offer a comprehensive cleaning experience. Attendants perform all the cleaning tasks, including hand washing, waxing, vacuuming the interior, and detailing.

Buffing

This is a process of smoothening or shining a surface using a rotating pad or wheel with an abrasive compound applied. It removes scratches, swirls, and other cosmetic issues from metal, plastic, wood, and even paint to create a smoother finish.

Interior Wash

The goal is to remove dirt, stains, odors, and contaminants from inside the vehicle's cabin to provide a clean and refreshed environment for the driver and passengers.

Vacuum Cleaning

his cleaning method is used to remove dirt, dust, debris, and other contaminants from the inside of a car, truck, or other types of vehicles. Car vacuum cleaning is an essential part of vehicle maintenance and helps to keep the interior of the vehicle clean and comfortable.
